About Me

Hello! Welcome to the vibrant mosaic of my career journey as a seasoned software engineer. My path began in a technology services company, where I steered multi-million dollar projects. This foundational experience was a springboard to the dynamic world of startups. Here, I became a key player in driving growth, scaling teams, and spearheading software engineering excellence. Excelling in various roles, I blend technical expertise with strategic foresight, continually integrating cutting-edge technologies to fulfill evolving company objectives.

Away from the tech world, my interests are vast and varied. I'm committed to leveraging technology in education and healthcare, aiming to make a real difference. Traveling with my family, exploring various places and cultures, and staying updated on global trends fuel my curiosity. On the field, my passion for cricket and volleyball comes alive, enjoying these sports both as a player and an enthusiast.

Currently, my focus is on the transformative potential of generative AI, exploring how it can enhance productivity and drive innovation. Concurrently, I'm developing educational tools, reflecting my belief in technology as a force for change. This journey is bolstered by my commitment to continuous learning, evidenced by pursuing certifications, each marking a milestone in my ongoing quest for knowledge and excellence.

Professional Experience

In my diverse and impactful career, I have taken on pivotal roles at various innovative companies. My tenure at Pindrop Security was marked by leading application and data platform development, aiding the enterprise's fight against voice fraud detection and authentication.
